Project Idea

Overview

Build an AI Dog translator

Dogs already communicate with humans in different ways, it is obvious to a human when a dog is Angry, scared, or sad given the distinctive, bark, howls or grown respectively, but wouldn’t it be cool to understand the nuances of exactly what your dog is trying to convey?

Motivation

Dogs make quite the companion, perhaps this is because they can’t talk. But if they could, it would enhance the dog owners experience to understand what our fury friends are really thinking and improve efficiency of figuring out problems when something is wrong or they are in pain.

Description

Product would be a downloadable app on a smartphone, which takes in audio from the microphone, recognises the subtleties in our K-9 friends bark or howl tones and pinpoint exactly what the dog is trying to say, the software would send the sound snippet to cloud infrastructure which could run the sound through its optimized AI system which would have been trained on hundreds of thousands of scenarios to give a result that is 95% accurate as to what the dog wants to say.

Tools and Technologies

With the ever-expanding power of AI, which is essentially a dynamic optimized search algorithm iterating re-weighted nodes, to find or even create optimal parameterization for some sort of framework in a system to achieve a specified result. In this case identify vocal tones of Dogs and how it relates to what they want to convey. The challenge with this is outcomes would still be highly subjective and require human input at each iteration (with current tech), this would make the process incredibly slow.

People/Skills Required

Phone APP – An application builder for Android and iPhone.

Cloud infrastructure – Someone who can set up cloud server with API through phone APP.

AI Specialist – Someone who knows how to build and train AI systems

Thousands of volunteer dog owners for testing data

Outcome

If the project were to be successful dogs would probably become key decision makers in society and given there highly empathetic nature, this would be good. Relationships may be strained when we find out what they really think of us.
