Project Idea: The Auto Programmer

Abstract

Recent major disruptions in society have led to dramatic shifts in how organisations are structuring their working environments, in particular an astonishing increase in the hybrid approach of working from home.

Motivation

This shift has led to a drastic change in how employees are approaching their work tasks, with many opting for more novel timesaving solutions, motivated to increase personal productivity to maximise personal leisure at home, perhaps this is the motivation that goes unsaid, although very much at the forefront of employee’s minds. This is evident in the recent popularisation of the phrase “automate yourself out of a job” seen on worker help forums across the internet, implying finding systems to effectively automate all or part of your job for you. This is a powerful ideological shift for employee-employer relations, as it moves the dynamic from a time transactional based system where the employer owns a share of your time, to a purely effectiveness-based transaction, where the employer cares about effectiveness of task completion, rather than ensuring you’re at your desk for 95% of your work hours.

The Idea of automating an IT position would be simple for say a data entry position, whereby you could program a simple macro to do what you would normally do manually, whilst obviously ensuring oversight for error prevention, these concepts are clearly displayed and employed by work from home staff according to various forums online. The challenge is presented when it comes to automating more complex assignments such as functional or objective programming.

Description

Wouldn’t it be cool to be able to type a couple of natural language sentences to specify intention and requirements, and have an automated system spit out the correct functional code to solve your requirements? to have an automated system which understands the nuanced composition of programming languages, comprehension, and compatibility of existing functional frameworks, including the massive array of opensource and built for purpose module library’s.

The crux of the issue would be to have an effective natural language processing system which can effectively interpret the users’ intensions, requirements, and specifications.

The next stage would be to organise all the available functions and combination of functions in a programming language and apply an intension tag, ie the purpose of the function. Then create a library of natural linguistic intentions with matched functional frameworks to achieve user input requirements, effectively designing a program to program itself.

What about problem solving? Humans tend to follow a framework of trial and error or deductive reasoning for problem solving, this could effectively be replaced with a computer program. The more complicated issue arises when trying to replicate the human capacity for inductive reasoning and abstract thinking, hopefully this will provide the human edge in a ‘Terminator’ style event.

For the purposes of this project, I do not believe problems requiring abstract human thinking can be easily replaced, and this program is not meant to solve abstract work problems.

Given that most entry level programming jobs would be tasked with creating applications with simple logical processes, the vast majority of which would already have been done in a similar opensource format, The Auto Programmer application would be ideal for increasing task completion effectiveness for these workers, thus increasing leisure time and arguably the user’s standard of living.

The Auto Programmer would be available to users on an entirely tax deductable subscription-based model, given it would be considered a productivity tool. The product would not be available to organisations directly, rather only to work from home employees to ensure congruency with the company mission statement and our core values.

Tools and Technologies

The application and backend systems would be cloud based to ensure the subscription model viability; security and encryption of the system would be of paramount importance.

The backend structuring of the frameworks would require robust yet dynamic databasing systems such as Oracle.

The Natural language processing system would probably be built off an AI model, or through licensing an existing NLP system with API functionality to our backend frameworks.

The application would be written in C++ and ideally will interpret frameworks to write other programming languages. We will call this process program-ception for pop-culture and marketing purposes.

People/Skills Required

This endeavour would require a sophisticated team of multi-disciplinary professionals skilled in databasing, NLP, AI, Open-source intelligence, programming, cloud deployment, marketing, and cyber security.

Databasing: A complex web of interconnected databases would be required to form the ‘brain’ of the program.

AI: Machine learning could be used to learn problem solving techniques mirroring a humans deductive reasoning process.

Open-source intelligence: the program would include a web scraper to identify and grab relevant open-source materials outside the scope of library modules.

Cybersecurity: business viability requires the system to be secure and closed source.

Outcome

The discourse between employer and employee has existed since the dawn of employment, the dynamics of this relationship is continually changing, particularly due to the shift in trend towards a more fluid and decentralised management style, where employees are more self-actualised, self-determined and are seeking greater autonomy.

The Auto Programmer would allow the user to increase their effectiveness and productive capacity whilst maximising their work satisfaction for billable hours, thus increasing their effective standard of living, which is the ultimate pursuit of wo-mankind over time.
